Honest Answers to Your Questions

No sales speak, just straight answers to what you really want to know.

Will this actually work for my auto shop?

Honest answer: It works for most auto shops, but not all. If you're in a very small town (under 10,000 people) or have no competition, you might not see dramatic results. But if you're in a normal market and currently don't have a good website, you'll likely see 20-40% more inquiries within 2 months.

How much technical knowledge do I need?

Honest answer: Almost none. We set everything up for you. You'll need to check emails for new appointments and maybe update your business hours once in a while. If you can use Facebook, you can manage this.

Are there any monthly fees?

Honest answer: No monthly fees for the website itself. However, the AI chat assistant costs $39/month after the first month (which is free). You can turn it off if you don't want it, and your website will still work perfectly.

What if I don't like it?

Honest answer: You get your money back within 30 days, no questions asked. You can even keep the website. We've only had 3 refund requests out of 247 websites built, and 2 of those were because the shop owner retired.

How is this different from other website companies?

Honest answer: Most charge monthly fees forever. Others give you a generic template. We build a custom website specifically for auto shops, include the AI assistant, and charge one price upfront. No ongoing payments unless you want the AI chat.

Can I make changes to the website later?

Honest answer: Yes, but we charge for major changes ($150/hour). Small updates like changing hours or adding services are free for the first month. After that, basic updates are $50 each.

What if my business is different or unique?

Honest answer: This works best for general auto repair shops. If you specialize in only exotic cars, motorcycles, or heavy trucks, it might not be the best fit. But if you do general auto repair, oil changes, brakes, etc., it's perfect.

How long does it really take to set up?

Honest answer: 2-5 business days typically. Sometimes faster if you get us all the information quickly. The longest we've taken is 8 days, and that was because the shop owner was on vacation and didn't respond to our emails.
